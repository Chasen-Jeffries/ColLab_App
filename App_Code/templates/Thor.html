Thor

Thor, a central character in Norse mythology, is one of the most widely recognized deities within the pantheon. The product of the union between Odin and Fjörgyn, he is often portrayed as a formidable and mighty combatant. With domains including thunder, lightning, brute strength, and guardianship, Thor is an embodiment of raw power and vigilant protection. His primary weapon is Mjolnir, a formidable hammer that grants him command over tempestuous weather and allows him to deliver devastating strikes upon his adversaries.
